Cryptic Crosswords

Here you can find my assortment of (rather poor) cryptic crosswords I've created. Feel free to try them, with the knowledge that they are made by an amateur and are probably a bit dodgy. Alternatively, you are also free to return to the safety of my homepage.

For the mathematicians/interested, I gave a talk at Manchester's Pure Postgraduate Seminar in November 2024 on solving Cryptic Crosswords and their combinatorics, which was voted the Talk of the Year by attendees (thank you!). You can view the slides of Moroccan birds, coots, risk unending snow storms for this talk? (13,2,10) here.

Please note: I am currently on a hiatus from making new crosswords. They may return in the future...
